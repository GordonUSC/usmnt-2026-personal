Getting to the stadium

SoFi, the easy way

SoFi Stadium, 1001 S Stadium Dr, Inglewood. The USA's LA matches play here. Parking sells out and traffic is real, so plan the approach.

🚆 Metro (smartest)

  • Take the Metro K Line to Downtown Inglewood, then the free stadium shuttle, or a ~12 min walk.
  • Buy a TAP card or use the TAP app; tap in and out.
  • Beats $60+ event parking and the post-match lot crawl.

🚗 Rideshare / car

  • Use the official rideshare drop zone, not the curb out front (it's gridlocked).
  • Driving: pre-buy parking online, arrive 90+ min early, expect a slow exit.
  • Pin your car's lot and row in your phone before you walk in.

Be part of it

The atmosphere

The crowd is half the experience. Here's how to be in it, not just at it.

📣 The American Outlaws

The USMNT's supporters' group. Find their section (usually behind a goal), learn the songs, and you're family for 90 minutes. "I believe that we will win" is the one everyone knows.

🧣 Bring a scarf

Hold it up with both hands during the anthem and the big moments. It's the universal soccer salute, and it photographs beautifully.

Know the other side

Türkiye, the opponent

Already eliminated after two losses, so expect them to play freely, with pride and nothing to lose. Watch these.

🇹🇷 Arda Güler

21, Real Madrid, the jewel. A left foot that can bend a pass or a free kick into anywhere. Worth the price of admission even in a dead rubber.

🇹🇷 Hakan Çalhanoğlu

The captain and metronome. Deep-lying playmaker, set-piece weapon. If Türkiye threatens, it runs through him.

Where to be

Seats & snacks

🎟️ Where to sit

  • Behind a goal = the loud, flag-waving supporter end. Best vibe.
  • Midfield, higher up = best view of the shape and the tactics.
  • Lower corners = closest to the action and the celebrations.

🌭 Snacks & lines

  • Eat before kickoff or at halftime's first minute, not the last.
  • SoFi is cashless, so have your phone/card ready.
  • Hydrate early; the LA evening is warm but the concourse is a trek.
From here, it's win or go home

The road to the Final

48 teams, so the first knockout is the Round of 32. Win and you advance: R32 → R16 → Quarter → Semi → Final (July 19, MetLife). One loss ends it.
