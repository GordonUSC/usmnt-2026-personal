Three matches · Twelve days · Two venues

Group D.

USA at home, favored at +140. Türkiye next at +200. Paraguay +425. Australia +900. The two who advance both leave LA.

Match 1 · Jun 12 · SoFi

Paraguay.

Paraguay (La Albirroja)
Coach Gustavo Alfaro · 4-2-3-1 / 4-4-2

Star: Miguel Almirón (Atlanta United pace, the counter-attack threat).

Key spine: Gómez (CB, captain), Alderete (CB), Cubas (DM), Enciso (creator), Sanabria (ST).

The shape

Defensive organization first. One of the stingiest defenses in CONMEBOL qualifying: 10 conceded in 18 matches. They scored just 14, the lowest among auto-qualifiers. They are built to frustrate, not to win 3-2.

How they hurt USA

  • Almirón on the counter when USA's fullbacks push too high
  • Set pieces in the second 45 when USA's back line is stretched
  • Patient, structured pressure that forces low-percentage passes

How USA wins

Patience. Don't force the early goal. Win the midfield. Trust Pulisic and Balogun to find one half-chance and convert. If 0-0 at 60, this becomes about who breaks character first.

Match 2 · Jun 19 · Lumen Field · Noon PT kickoff

Australia.

Australia (Socceroos)
Coach Tony Popovic · 3-4-2-1

Star: Nestory Irankunda (pace, finishing, breakout player).

Key spine: Ryan (GK), Souttar / Burgess / Circati (back 3), Bos (LWB), McGree (creator).

The shape

Settled back three. Wing-backs are the engine. They press the full 90 minutes and they don't fade. Beat Denmark and Tunisia at Qatar 2022. Reached the R16. Tony Popovic took over in the middle of a crisis and went unbeaten through qualification.

How they hurt USA

  • Pressing intensity forcing USA into errors in the build
  • Irankunda's pace against any unsettled centerback
  • Set-piece moments through their back-three height

How USA wins

Match the press intensity. Use Adams and McKennie to bypass the first wave. Make the wing-backs defend by pinning them with Robinson and Weah. Australia is dangerous as long as they're moving. Make them defend their box.

Match 3 · Jun 25 · SoFi · 7pm PT kickoff

Türkiye.

Türkiye (Ay-Yıldızlılar)
Coach Vincenzo Montella · 4-2-3-1

Star: Arda Güler (Real Madrid, 21, the truth).

Key spine: Çakır (GK), Demiral / Bardakçı (CB), Çalhanoğlu (deep playmaker), Güler (no. 10), Yıldız (LW).

The shape

Creative spark via Güler between the lines. Çalhanoğlu sets tempo from deep and runs the best dead-ball routines on the continent. Yıldız stretches the back line on the left. They came through European playoffs and look sharp.

How they hurt USA

  • Çalhanoğlu set pieces (corners, indirect free kicks)
  • Güler between the lines if USA's midfield drops too deep
  • Yıldız one-vs-one against any non-Robinson fullback

How USA wins

Press Çalhanoğlu. Don't let him pick his head up. Adams or McKennie has to be in his lap for 90 minutes. If Çalhanoğlu plays free, Türkiye scores twice.

What this game means

If both sides come in tied on points, this is the actual group-decider. Winning Group D matters for the R32 path. Easier 3rd-place opponent if we win, harder if we don't.
